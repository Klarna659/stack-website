FAQ

Quick answers

Is Stack free?

Yes. Tracking, reminders, supply projection, streaks and weight trends are free — with no cap on how many compounds you track. Plus ($9.99/mo or $49.99/year) adds the insight layer: in-system level curves, bloodwork ranges, your full trend history, cloud backup and unlimited Sage. Or get Lifetime once ($124.99) and keep it all forever.

Do I need an account?

You sign in with just your email — no password — so your Plus and settings follow you across devices. But the account only ever knows your email, never what you take: your health data stays encrypted on your device, and cloud backup is opt-in. You can always export an encrypted backup file you own.

What can I track?

Anything you take on a schedule: supplements, vitamins, medications, peptides, GLP-1s and TRT — plus habits, weight, lifts and bloodwork alongside your doses.

Does Stack give dosing advice?

No. Stack is a logging tool and an educational reference. It never recommends compounds or doses, and nothing in the app or on this site is medical advice.

When does it launch?

Android first, very soon — iOS after. Email us and we'll tell you the moment it's live.
