A small introduction

Stack is a quiet place to track everything you take.

Supplements, peptides, GLP-1, vitamins, prescriptions — a private, on-device log that remembers every dose, every refill, every day.

What it does

You add a compound, and Stack handles the rest — schedule, half-life, refill projection, an encrypted record you can export at any time. Scan a label and the app pre-fills the math.

What it doesn't

Stack will not tell you what to take. It will not flag a dose as "good" or "bad." It will not sell your data, because there is none to sell — every byte lives on your phone, encrypted with a key only you hold.
