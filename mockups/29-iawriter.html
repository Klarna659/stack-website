What it does

Stack remembers every dose. It models each compound's half-life so the schedule actually fits the chemistry, not a generic 8 a.m. reminder. It projects when your vial runs out. It draws an honest 30-day trend, never a verdict.

What it doesn't

Stack will not coach you. It will not score you. It will not sell your data, because there is none to sell — every byte lives on your phone, encrypted with a key only you hold.
