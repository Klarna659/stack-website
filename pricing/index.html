Questions

Good to know

Where do I buy Plus?

Right here on the web. Then open the app and sign in with the same email — Plus is already on, nothing to restore. Buying on the web is how we skip the app-store cut and keep the price honest.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Monthly and annual are cancel-anytime, and you keep Plus until the period you paid for ends. Lifetime is a one-time purchase — yours forever, no subscription.

What's actually free?

The whole logbook: every compound with no caps, reminders, supply projection, streaks, milestones, injection rotation, weight trend and label scanning, plus 3 Sage questions a day. Plus adds the insight layer — the curves, labs and long trends.

Do I need an account to use the app?

You sign in so Plus can follow you across devices and the web. Your health data stays encrypted on your device; cloud backup is opt-in.

What's the difference between the plans' price?

Monthly is $9.99. Annual is $49.99 — that's about 58% off the monthly rate, roughly $0.14 a day. Lifetime is $124.99 once and never renews.
