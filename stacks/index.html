Share your stack

Stack turns your whole protocol into a single private code or QR. Send it to whoever you want — a friend who's starting out, a partner running the same thing — and they can open it to see exactly what you take. Nobody else can.

01

Generate

Tap share on any stack in the app. Stack packs it into an encrypted code and a QR.

02

Send

Text it, DM it, hand someone the QR — only the people you give it to can open it.

03

They view

They scan the QR or paste the code into Stack and see your protocol. On their terms, at their discretion.

No public directory, no feeds, no leaderboard of what anyone takes. A shared stack is just a snapshot you chose to send — it isn't tied to your identity, and it never passes through our servers (we don't have any). How your data stays yours →
